
# Duskwaker [Working Title]

An in development life-sim with horror elements focusing on relationship mechanics with your superior in a dark techno-fantasy world. 

Gameplay loop consists of Voices of the Void-like maintenance and data relaying of in-universe analogue radars, outposts, and the main base; STALKER/Pacific Drive-like exploration and scrapping of anomalous structures, ruined installations, and other set pieces that focus on high danger high reward incentivization; combat avoidance and general stealth/creative problem solving as enemies are purposefully too durable without either assistance of your superior or superstantual circumstances.
